Product Description

The Mansions of Madness Player Board is designed to enhance your gameplay experience by providing a dedicated organization system for your investigator's components.

Each board features laser-etched artwork inspired by the Lovecraftian themes of the game, with clearly defined spaces for your investigator card, health and sanity tokens, and various card types you'll acquire during your adventures.

The premium 3mm birch plywood construction ensures durability while maintaining a lightweight feel, and the clear lacquer finish protects the wood while highlighting its natural beauty.

The board's layout has been carefully designed based on extensive gameplay testing to provide the most intuitive organization of your investigator's components. The etched labels ensure you'll never forget where each card type belongs.
