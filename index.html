The Craftsman Behind The Board Viking

Hello! I'm the sole creator behind every product at The Board Viking. As a passionate board gamer myself, I noticed that many games could benefit from better organization and specialized player aids.

I started designing laser-cut solutions for my own collection, focusing on what I believed would work best for each game. Soon, friends and fellow gamers began requesting my designs for their own collections.

Today, I personally design, cut, and finish each item in my workshop. I only create organizers and accessories for games I own and play, ensuring that every product is tested and refined through actual gameplay experience.
